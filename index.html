I am an assistant professor in the Computer Science Department at Emory University. My research is in data mining and machine learning, focusing on healthcare applications. I received my PhD from UT Austin, M.S. and B.S. from MIT. I co-founded a successful healthcare analytics company (Accordion Health) and previously worked at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.
